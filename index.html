About US

Burka Group is majorly a construction company established in 2012. Over years the Group has undertaken many challenging projects and accumulated skills, know-how and experience in design and build solutions, project management services , building trades and related engineering works.

Today, BURKA Group is not only a big construction company but also deals with Transportation of materials especially building and construction materials, offers Laundrymart services and General cleaniness services. Our objective is to provide our clients with "Am assured" experience.
